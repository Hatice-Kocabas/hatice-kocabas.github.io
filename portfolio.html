Basket Analysis

This project delves into sales data from one of the LCW's store, spanning January 1, 2019, to December 31, 2019, encompassing a vast dataset of 2,122,720 records. Focused on one LC Waikiki store, the annual sales data is analyzed to understand customer shopping behavior. Leveraging the Apriori algorithm, the project explores customer preferences by suggesting additional products based on items added to their carts. With approximately two million data points, this application offers insights into potential purchases, providing a valuable tool for enhancing customer experience and optimizing sales strategies. 🛍️📈

Milk Quality Prediction

This project introduces a machine learning model to swiftly assess milk quality, combating overfitting challenges through early stopping. Utilizing key factors like pH and temperature, a deep neural network classifies milk into three quality levels with 91.04% accuracy on the test set. The solution prioritizes efficient development using tools like Google Colab, PyTorch, and Jupyter Notebooks. Beyond its tech roots, the project aims to enhance food safety, cut waste, and optimize dairy production processes. With an IoT dataset at its core, the system is poised for real-time milk quality predictions, adding a futuristic dimension to its impact. 🌐🚀🔮

Automated Ventilation

Aligned with the 4th Sustainable Development Goal, our project introduces an automated ventilation system to enhance the quality of education by prioritizing air quality in classrooms. Using an MQ-135 air sensor and Arduino, we monitor CO2 levels, triggering the ventilation system when thresholds are exceeded. The system, accessible online, dynamically adjusts fan speed based on real-time air quality data. Future plans include integrating machine learning for optimized responses. Referencing Bogdanovica et al.'s study on CO2's impact on student well-being during learning, the project aims to create a healthier and more productive learning environment.

Task Manager
