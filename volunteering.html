AGU COMPUTER SOCIETY - BOARD MEMBER

AGU Computer Society is one of the student clubs at Abdullah Gül University. This club organizes various activities for students, including training sessions on various algorithms, machine learning, and other related topics. In addition to these training sessions, the club collaborates with other communities to organize major events. For example, we partnered with the Computer Society to host this year's Kayseri DevFest event. I have been serving as a voluntary board member since December 2023.
